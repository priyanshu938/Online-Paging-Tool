About

This is an Online Page Replacement tool developed by Priyanshu Tiwari,

a Computer Science Engineering student of Amity University Lucknow Campus.

There are four types of Page Replacement Algorithm-

1) FIFO

2) LRU

3) LFU

4)OPTIMAL

How to use?

1) First enter the number of frames.

2) Then enter the page reference string.

3) Click on the any of the four types of buttons to get the desired output.
